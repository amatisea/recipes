Dark Chocolate Nut Clusters with Sea Salt
2 cups of dark chocolate chips
1 1/2 cuts of unsalted nuts
sea salt

In a small saucepan, melt chocolate in a double broiler over low heat. Stir in chopped nuts and add a pinch of sea salt. Spoon into clusters on parchment paper. 

Place in refrigerator until colled and set.
